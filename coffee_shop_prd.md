# Coffee E-Commerce Website - Product Requirements Document

## Project Overview
Create a clean, minimal, and warm-toned e-commerce website for a specialty coffee brand. The site should prioritize high-quality product imagery, easy navigation, and a seamless shopping experience. It is a product-focused storefront with an events section.

**Reference style:** `momoscoffee.net` – simple layout, neutral and warm color palette (whites, creams, beiges), generous white space, clean product grids, modern typography.

## Site Structure & Navigation
Four main pages, accessible from a sticky top navigation bar:
1. **Home**
2. **About Us**
3. **Shop**
4. **Cart**

## Global Design & Technical Requirements
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, clean, modern with a warm coffee-shop feel. 
- **Palette:** Whites, creams, beiges (e.g., `#FFFFFF`, `#F5F0EB`, `#4A3B32`).
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif like Inter or Lato.
- **Responsiveness:** Mobile, tablet, desktop.
- **Interactivity:** Sticky nav, cart badge, filtering on Shop page, localStorage for cart state.

## Detailed Page Requirements

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero:** Full-width coffee-related image with subtle text overlay.
- **Categories:** Side-by-side cards for "Green Bean" and "Roasted Bean" with "Shop Now" links.
- **Events Section:** "Upcoming Events" with cards showing image, date, title, and description.

### 2. Shop Page
- **Filtering:** Simple filter bar (All | Green Bean | Roasted Bean).
- **Product Grid:** 2-3 columns on desktop, 1 on mobile.
- **Product Card:** Image, name, price, "Add to Cart" button.

### 3. Product Detail Page
- **Layout:** Large product image, details (roast, notes, origin), quantity selector, and "Add to Cart".

### 4. Cart Page
- **Content:** List of items with image, name, quantity controls, price, and removal.
- **Summary:** Subtotal and "Proceed to Checkout".

### 5. Checkout Page
- **Layout:** Two-column (Form on left, Summary on right).
- **Fields:** Contact, Shipping, Payment (placeholders).
- **Confirmation:** Success message after "Place Order".

### 6. About Us Page
- **Content:** Brand story, mission, sourcing philosophy, and high-quality imagery of the roasting process.
